Version 1.0.0 • macOS 13.0+ • Apple Silicon & Intel

Note for macOS Users

Since I am an independent developer and not paying Apple's enterprise fees, your Mac may falsely flag this app as "damaged" to protect you. It is not damaged.

To run the app:

  1. Download and open the DMG. Drag the app to your Applications folder.
  2. Open your Terminal app.
  3. Paste this code and hit Enter:
    xattr -cr /Applications/TextFoundry.app

You can now open the app normally!
