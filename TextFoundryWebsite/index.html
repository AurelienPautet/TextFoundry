Frequently Asked Questions

Bring Your Own Keys

TextFoundry is a BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) application. This means you use your own API keys from providers like OpenAI, Google Gemini, or xAI. This ensures you pay the lowest possible price directly to the provider and maintain full control over your usage limits.

Local Models with LM Studio

Prefer to keep everything offline? TextFoundry integrates seamlessly with LM Studio. Just start your local server in LM Studio, and TextFoundry can route all corrections through your local LLM for complete privacy and zero cost.

Customizable Shortcuts

Workflow is everything. You can customize the global hotkey (default Cmd+Shift+E) to whatever suits your muscle memory. You can also assign specific hotkeys to individual custom prompts for lightning-fast access to your most used corrections.
